About the CR Program

Mood Indigo, IIT Bombay's annual cultural festival has grown to become Asia's largest college cultural festival. Over the years, it has seen a consistent increase in the footfall and college participation with 1,31,000 students visiting the festival from over 1650 colleges from all over the world in Mood Indigo 2015.

Mood Indigo's CR system plays an integral part in its growth and serves as a major platform for distribution of information. It helps Mood Indigo in providing opportunities to the talented students from colleges all over the country. A College Representative will be a key element in fulfilling this vision in Mood Indigo 2016.

Why college representative

Management

Improve your creative and management skills to organise cultural events in your college in collaboration with Mood Indigo, IIT Bombay.

Communication

An opportunity to enhance communication skills as you get to interact with other College Representatives and students from your college.

Prizes

A chance to receive a certificate of appreciation for being an integral part of Mood Indigo 2016, IIT Bombay as well as various exciting prizes worth INR 3 lacs.

Your responsibilities as a CR

Social Media

Publicising our events on various social media platforms by sharing our posts in your college groups etc.

On Ground Publicity

Putting up posters of Mood Indigo on your respective college boards within 4 days of receiving it.

Coordination

Coordinating with the Mood Indigo team and helping organise Multicity events.

Incentives
